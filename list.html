What is<LIST>


HTML lists are used to present list of information in well formed and semantic way.
There are three different types of list in HTML and each one has a specific purpose and meaning.
  • <li> Ordered list — Used to create a list of related items, in a specific order.
  • <ul> Unordered list — Used to create a list of related items, in no particular order.
  • <dl> Definition lists — Used to create a list of terms and their descriptions.

Definition List:


Stuxnet
is an extremely sophisticated computer worm that exploits multiple previously unknown
Windows zero-day vulnerabilities to infect computers and spread.

Duqu 2.0
is a highly sophisticated malware platform exploiting up to three zero-day vulnerabilities with
infections linked to the P5+1 events and venues for high level meetings between world leaders.

OceanLotus/APT32
is a hacker group associated with the government of Vietnam.
It has been accused of Cyberespionage of political dissidents, businesses and foreign
officials that have ties to Vietnam.

Cobalt Strike
is threat emulation software that executes targeted attacks against modern enterprises
with one of the most powerful network attack kits available to penetration testers.
It is currently used by more cybercrime and general commodity malware operators
than APT and espionage threat actors.
